*Live streaming video is now available every day of the week from the
 International Space Station. The video will show views of Earth and
 the exterior structure of the station, as seen from cameras mounted
 outside the ISS, and other times, activities going on inside the
 station. If you regularly watch [NASA
 TV](http://www.nasa.gov/multimedia/nasatv/index.html) online, just go
 to the same website, and now there's another choice of channels. Just
 click on the "[Live Space Station
 Video](http://www.nasa.gov/multimedia/nasatv/index.html)" tab to
 enjoy.*

[via [Universe Today](http://www.universetoday.com/2009/03/10/live-from-space-streaming-webcam-now-available/)]

Funktioniert auch wunderbar hier unter Linux (Konqueror mit Kaffeine Plugin - Codecs von [debian-multimedia](http://debian-multimedia.org/), und das statische Rauschen ist auch beruhigend :-)
