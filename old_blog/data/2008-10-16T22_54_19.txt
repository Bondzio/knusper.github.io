*[Phlix.TV](http://phlix.tv/) basically will take any non-flash video file [AVI/MOV/WMV/MP4/Etc, as long as it's on a http server], and convert it to flash on the fly, without [too] much wait [5-15 seconds at most if the server containing the video is slow]. No need to download some random codec to watch videos, as long as you can stand [slightly higher quality than normal] flash video.*

Und laut Aussage des Entwicklers hat die Entwicklung des Web-Services dank [ffmpeg](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ffmpeg) 1 Wochenende gedauert.
