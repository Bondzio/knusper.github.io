Sometimes pitch black is not what’s directly outside a subway car’s window. You may notice an empty, dark, dusty platform as your train speeds forward. Or you might see the tunnel’s wall switch from cast iron tube to a brick wall. In major subway systems throughout the world, there are abandoned stations that one wouldn’t necessarily notice unless one were to be staring outside the subway window.
