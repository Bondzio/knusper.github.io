Knuspermix 2014-10 / 5 - Tidal Debris

My entry for the “Discogs Mix Competition 2014”. Is it good? Well, according to one commenter on soundcloud:

Pretty much faultless. A good reprosentation across a few modern subgenres with a smattering of really nice acid. At least three records in there I would sell my granny for and a good display of restraint giving the mix overall cohesion. I get the impression that this DJ had good knoweledge of the roots of electronic music in general. 9 out of 10.
